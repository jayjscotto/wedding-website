Our Story

Jason and Katherine met their freshman year of college. We... wouldn't exactly say it was love at first sight. Although, from the time they started dating their in their third year of school, Jason knew this would be permanent. Katherine and Jason kept their relationship going after they graduated, Jason moving into the professional world and Katherine staying at grad school. They went from living two doors away, to living almost an entire two miles away. The long distance was rough. Eventually, after moving again (this time into different states), Jay moved down to New Jersey to finally move in with Katherine. They currently reside in High Bridge with their dog, Cooper.
