Our Story

Jason and Katherine met their freshman year of college, both playing key roles on the Springfield College Swimming Team. It is important to note however, it wasn't exactly love at first sight. While the debate over who couldn't resist whose charm is still ongoing, Jason and Katherine have been together ever since they started dating Junior year. They have been there for each other every step of the way- finals, graduations, graduate school, new jobs, relocating, and long distance.

On the 5th of May 2018, Jason and Katherine left the pup at home, and went on a hike up New Jersey's famous Point Mountain. Katherine had no idea what was coming! Katherine, who moves like a gazelle on any surface, was cruising up the mountain with ease. Jay on the other hand, was sucking wind. Eventually after a long trek, they made it to the top. With almost no clouds in the sky and perfect weather, Jay finally got down on one knee after five years of dating and proposed to Katherine. Spoiler alert: she said, "Yes!"

Jason and Katherine currently reside in High Bridge, New Jersey with their incredibly handsome well-behaved, verified best boy, Cooper the goldendoodle.
